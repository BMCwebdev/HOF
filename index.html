The Copley Collection.

The Hearts On Fire Copley Collection, designed to reflect Boston’s historic beauty, was born from the inspiration new Italian designer Ilaria Lanzoni felt during her first few weeks living in Boston. The city’s stunning terraces and European inspired ironwork architecture mesmerized Ilaria, who went on to create the Copley Collection with a focus on the true perfection and artistry of diamond jewelry
